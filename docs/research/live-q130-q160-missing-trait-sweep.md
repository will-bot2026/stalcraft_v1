## Notes

- Rubik was attempted in the first missing-trait sweep but the live Artifact panel produced no visible artifact stat rows in this baseline state; do not use that attempt as formula proof.

- Polyhedron special/mechanic stats (`Reduces damage by`, `Reload`, `Charge required to activate`, `Triggers when`) are now source-truthed but should be modeled as special-case mechanics, not normal always-on build stats.

- Positive displayed stats continue to quality-scale. For beneficial negative stats such as Recoil/Sway/negative Temperature, the more-beneficial endpoint scales with quality.

- Harmful rarity-threshold stats choose endpoints by rarity at threshold: Special/Exclusive = worse endpoint, Rare/Legendary = better endpoint, and do not quality-scale.
